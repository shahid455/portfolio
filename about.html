About Me

Hi there! My name is Shahidul Hasan.

I am 20 years old and currently a 2nd-year Computer Science

Engineering (CSE) student at SRM Institute of Science and

Technology (SRMIST), Kattankulathur. Hailing from

Uttar Pradesh, I come from a supportive family where my father

is a land owner and my mother is a homemaker.

From a young age, I have valued punctuality and discipline.

I am a strong-willed individual who approaches every task

with utmost sincerity, dedication, and passion.

I chose to join SRMIST to benefit from the high-quality

education and excellent facilities that the institute offers.

My goal is to play a significant role in the tech society and

to perform all my tasks to the best of my abilities. I am driven by the

aspiration to contribute meaningfully to the field of technology

and continually strive for excellence.
